Privacy Policy

Personal details should stay personal.

Thoughtfully is built for birthdays, saved details, and the small reminders that help people feel remembered.

01

What you save

Thoughtfully lets you save names, birthdays, relationships, reminder choices, favorite details, gift clues, personal notes, profile photos, and meaningful moments.

This information is stored locally on your device unless otherwise stated. Thoughtfully does not sell personal information.

02

Permissions

Thoughtfully only requests permissions when they are needed for a feature you choose to use.

Notifications Birthday reminders, saved moment reminders, and gentle check-ins.

Photos Used only when you choose a profile photo.

Contacts Used only when you import one selected contact. Thoughtfully imports available name, birthday, contact photo, and notes only.

03

Thoughtful Ideas

Thoughtfully can use selected saved profile details to generate helpful suggestions for birthdays, gifts, and small ways to show up. This feature can be turned off anytime in Settings.

When this feature is used, only selected profile details needed to create suggestions are used. Thoughtfully does not save those idea requests to a separate app database.

04

Affiliate disclosure

As an Amazon Associate, Thoughtfully may earn from qualifying purchases at no extra cost to you.
